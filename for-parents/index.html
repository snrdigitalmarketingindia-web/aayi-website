For babies under 3 months any fever above 38°C warrants a call to your pediatrician right away. Questions

What parents ask most

Is the vaccine schedule on Aayi.ai accurate for India? ⌄

Yes. The vaccine schedule is aligned to Indian Academy of Pediatrics (IAP) guidelines and the National Immunization Schedule (NIS). Your pediatrician can also customise the schedule based on local recommendations or any catch-up vaccines needed.

Does aayi Companion replace a call to my pediatrician? ⌄

No — and it's not designed to. aayi Companion handles the high-frequency, routine questions that come at all hours. If it detects anything that needs clinical attention, it alerts your pediatrician immediately. Think of it as a filter — the genuinely urgent calls still get through.

Can both parents use the same Aayi.ai account? ⌄

Yes. Both parents can access the same child profile on Aayi.ai. Vaccine reminders and milestone updates are shared between both accounts so neither parent misses an alert.

Up to what age does Aayi.ai track child health? ⌄

Aayi.ai covers child health from birth to age 12, covering the full primary vaccination schedule, developmental milestones, school health checks, and common childhood illnesses.
