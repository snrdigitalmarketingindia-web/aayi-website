Technical Operations Engineer
(DevOps · QA · AI Tools)
Full-time Hyderabad / Remote 0–3 yrs experience
Actively Hiring
About the Role

You'll be the person who keeps Aayi's product running reliably — finding bugs before users do, debugging issues across the stack, and making sure every new release ships cleanly. This is a hands-on role that sits at the intersection of software quality, AI-assisted operations, and technical communication. You'll work directly with the development team and report on product health in real time.

What You'll Do
  • Understand and document the full software development lifecycle — from feature spec to release
  • Test the Aayi app (Android & iOS) end-to-end for bugs, regressions, and edge cases
  • Identify, reproduce, and report issues with clear steps and severity ratings
  • Analyse real-time incidents — crashes, API failures, sync issues — and escalate with structured reports
  • Write and maintain test cases, test plans, and QA checklists
  • Use AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini) and prompt engineering to speed up testing, documentation, and debugging workflows
  • Monitor deployments and flag performance or reliability concerns before they reach users
  • Collaborate with developers to verify bug fixes and close issues
What We're Looking For
  • Understanding of the software development lifecycle (SDLC) and agile workflows
  • Basic development or scripting skills — enough to read code, reproduce bugs, and write simple test scripts
  • Strong debugging mindset — you don't stop at "it's broken", you find out why
  • Experience or strong interest in AI tools and prompt engineering
  • Ability to write clear, structured bug reports and technical summaries
  • Comfort with real-time issue analysis — reading logs, tracing API calls, interpreting error messages
  • Familiarity with testing tools (Postman, browser DevTools, mobile testing) is a plus
  • A healthcare or health-tech interest is a bonus, not a requirement
Nice to Have
  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines, GitHub Actions, or deployment workflows
  • Familiarity with Android/iOS testing tools (Maestro, Appium, XCUITest)
  • Basic knowledge of SQL or JSON for data verification
  • Previous experience in a startup or early-stage product environment
What You'll Get
  • Direct access to the founding team and real product ownership
  • Work on a product used by real mothers and doctors across India
  • Hands-on exposure to AI-native workflows and modern dev tools
  • Flexible hours and a collaborative, non-hierarchical team culture
  • Competitive compensation based on experience

Social Media & Content Manager
(Digital Marketing · Video · Web)
Full-time Hyderabad / Remote 1–4 yrs experience
Actively Hiring
About the Role

You'll own Aayi's social media presence and content pipeline end to end — from writing posts to editing short-form videos to keeping the website up to date. This is a creative and strategic role for someone who understands both storytelling and distribution. You'll also manage content for our partner clients, so multi-tasking across brands is part of the job. Prompt engineering and AI-assisted content tools will be a core part of your daily workflow.

What You'll Do
  • Plan, create, and publish content across Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, and LinkedIn for Aayi and client accounts
  • Write compelling captions, carousels, blogs, and scripts — in English, Hindi, and/or Telugu
  • Edit short-form videos and reels (trending formats, educational content, product demos)
  • Maintain and update the Aayi website — adding new pages, updating copy, refreshing sections as the product evolves
  • Review and proofread all outgoing content for accuracy, tone, and brand consistency
  • Use AI tools and prompt engineering to produce high-quality content faster — text, image, and video
  • Track performance metrics (reach, engagement, follower growth) and share weekly reports
  • Coordinate with the founding team on campaigns, product launches, and announcements
What We're Looking For
  • 1–4 years of experience in social media management or digital marketing
  • Strong writing skills — you can switch between formal (doctor-facing) and warm (mom-facing) tones
  • Basic to intermediate video editing skills (Reels, Shorts — tools like CapCut, Premiere, or similar)
  • Comfortable making minor website edits (HTML/CSS basics or CMS experience — no deep coding needed)
  • Experience using AI tools for content creation (ChatGPT, Claude, Canva AI, etc.)
  • Attention to detail — you review before you post
  • Able to manage multiple client accounts without mixing up brand voices
  • Understanding of maternal health or healthcare content is a strong plus
Nice to Have
  • Experience running paid ad campaigns (Meta Ads, Google Ads)
  • Familiarity with analytics tools (Meta Business Suite, Google Analytics, YouTube Studio)
  • Content creation in Telugu or Hindi for regional health audiences
  • Photography or graphic design skills (Canva, Photoshop, Figma)
What You'll Get
  • Full creative ownership of a growing health-tech brand's online presence
  • Work on meaningful content that reaches expecting mothers and doctors across India
  • Learn AI-native content workflows used at the cutting edge of digital marketing
  • Flexible hours with a collaborative, supportive team
  • Competitive compensation based on experience and portfolio
