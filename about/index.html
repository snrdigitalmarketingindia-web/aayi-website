Our Story

Built to close the gap that costs lives.

Between a doctor's 15-minute consultation and the 30 days after, mothers make hundreds of health decisions alone. Aayi.ai was built to make sure those decisions are right.

"Every mother deserves a doctor who never sleeps. Every doctor deserves a platform that lets them focus on medicine — not paperwork."
— The founding vision behind Aayi.ai
The Problem We Saw

The 30-day blind spot in maternal care

A typical OB-GYN consultation lasts 15 minutes. The next appointment is 30 days away. In between, mothers Google every symptom, send midnight WhatsApp messages to their doctors, and make critical health decisions without clinical guidance.

Doctors, meanwhile, spend 40% of their day on admin, receive unpaid teleconsult messages at all hours, and walk into each appointment with no data on what actually happened in the 30 days between visits.

This blind spot is dangerous. It leads to missed screenings, non-compliance with prenatal vitamins, delayed vaccine schedules, and outcomes that could have been prevented.

What We Built

The Solution

Aayi.ai is a doctor-first platform that connects the dots between clinic visits. Everything the mother does in the app — daily health logging, compliance tracking, aayi Companion questions — flows directly to the treating doctor's clinical dashboard.
