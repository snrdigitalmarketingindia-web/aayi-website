Frequently Asked Questions

Everything doctors, hospitals, and healthcare administrators ask before getting started with Aayi.ai.

What is Aayi.ai and how does it help doctors? ⌄

Aayi.ai is an AI-powered Maternal and Child Digital Ecosystem that bridges the gap between a 15-minute doctor consultation and 30 days of critical patient choices at home. It provides OB-GYN doctors, Pediatricians, and hospitals with AI tools to reduce admin work by 40%, improve patient compliance, and deliver continuous care between clinic visits — eliminating midnight calls and fragmented patient histories.

How does AI Saheli work for pregnant patients? ⌄

AI Saheli is Aayi.ai's 24/7 AI health companion built for mothers. When a patient has a question about pregnancy symptoms, nutrition, or what's normal at a given trimester, AI Saheli provides an evidence-based answer immediately — rather than the patient turning to Google or calling the doctor at midnight. The AI is curated by medical professionals and aligned to NMC and WHO guidelines.

Is Aayi.ai available for doctors in the UAE and Gulf countries? ⌄

Yes. Aayi.ai is available globally. It is currently active across India, the Gulf region (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman), and expanding to Southeast Asia, Africa, and the UK. The platform supports English, Hindi, Telugu, and Arabic, with more languages planned.

How does Aayi.ai improve patient compliance during pregnancy? ⌄

Aayi.ai's Compliance Tracker sends automated daily reminders for prenatal vitamins, diet requirements, trimester screening schedules, and lifestyle recommendations — all configured by the treating doctor. Patients log their compliance in under 2 minutes per day. This data flows directly to the doctor's dashboard, giving a real-time view of adherence without any manual follow-up calls.

How secure is patient data on the Aayi.ai platform? ⌄

All patient data on Aayi.ai is encrypted end-to-end using AES-256 encryption. The platform follows a consent-first architecture where patients explicitly control what is shared, with whom, and for how long. Aayi.ai is compliant with India's DPDP Act and the UAE's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL). No patient data is used for advertising or sold to third parties.

How can my clinic or hospital start using Aayi.ai? ⌄

Start with a free 20-minute demo where our team walks through your clinic workflow and customises Aayi for your specialty. We offer a pilot programme for qualifying clinics where you can run Aayi with a subset of patients before committing. Book your demo by filling the form on the homepage or emailing hello@aayi.ai.

Does Aayi.ai work in areas with poor internet connectivity? ⌄

Yes. The Aayi.ai mobile app is built with an offline-first architecture designed for semi-urban India where internet connectivity can be intermittent. Core features — daily health logging, checklists, AI Saheli cached responses, and reminders — work without active internet. Data automatically syncs to the doctor's dashboard when connectivity is restored.

What makes Aayi.ai different from other pregnancy apps? ⌄

Most pregnancy apps are consumer-only — they give information to mothers but don't connect back to the doctor. Aayi.ai is a doctor-first platform: everything the mother does in the app (compliance logging, symptom tracking, AI Saheli interactions) flows directly to the treating doctor's clinical dashboard. This closes the loop between patient behaviour and clinical decision-making — no other maternal health platform does this at the depth Aayi.ai does.
