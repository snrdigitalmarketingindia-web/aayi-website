What aayi Companion Does

Your doctor's knowledge, available between visits.

Between clinic appointments, expectant mothers have hundreds of questions. Is this symptom normal? Can I eat this? Should I be worried? Without access to clinical guidance, they turn to Google — and often get conflicting, scary, or wrong answers.

aayi Companion changes that. Built into the Aayi app, it's a safe, medically curated Q&A companion that answers questions with the same quality of guidance a doctor would give — drawn from WHO, NMC, and international clinical guidelines.

And because it's integrated with the doctor's dashboard, anything flagged by aayi Companion as needing clinical attention is automatically surfaced to the treating doctor — no midnight calls needed.

FAQ

About aayi Companion

What is aayi Companion?⌄
aayi Companion is Aayi.ai's 24/7 maternal health AI companion. It answers pregnancy and child health questions with evidence-based, WHO-guideline-aligned responses curated by qualified OB-GYN doctors. It's available in the Aayi app and is configured by the patient's treating doctor.
Is aayi Companion a substitute for a doctor?⌄
No. aayi Companion is an informational support tool, not a diagnostic or clinical decision-making tool. All clinical decisions must be made by a licensed healthcare professional. aayi Companion helps mothers understand their health between visits and reduces unnecessary anxiety — it does not replace medical advice.
What languages does aayi Companion support?⌄
aayi Companion currently supports English, Hindi, and Telugu, with more Indian and regional languages in development. The platform roadmap includes Arabic, Tamil, Kannada, and Marathi for 2026.
Can aayi Companion work without internet?⌄
Frequently asked questions and pre-loaded health content are accessible offline. Live AI responses require a data connection, but the app is optimised for low-bandwidth environments — it works reliably on 2G/3G connections.
How is patient privacy protected?⌄
All aayi Companion conversations are end-to-end encrypted. They are shared only with the patient's treating doctor — with explicit patient consent. Data is never used for advertising or sold to third parties. Aayi.ai is fully compliant with India's DPDP Act 2023 and UAE PDPL.
