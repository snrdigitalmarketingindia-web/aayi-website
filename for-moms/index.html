aayi Companion in Action

Real questions. Real answers. Instantly.

You asked
I'm 18 weeks and I've been having lower back pain since yesterday. Is this normal?
aayi Companion
Lower back pain at 18 weeks is very common and usually a normal part of pregnancy. Your growing uterus is shifting your centre of gravity, and the ligaments in your pelvis are softening (called relaxin). Try resting, a warm compress, and avoiding sitting for long periods. If the pain is severe, one-sided, or comes with fever, bleeding, or difficulty urinating — contact your doctor right away. I've noted this for your doctor's review at your next appointment. 🤱
You asked
Can I eat paneer during pregnancy?
aayi Companion
Yes! Paneer is an excellent source of protein and calcium during pregnancy. It's safe to eat as long as it's made from pasteurised milk and cooked through. Avoid raw or uncooked paneer from unknown sources. It's a great addition to your pregnancy diet — your doctor's meal plan has more details. 🧀

Questions

What expecting moms ask most

Is Aayi.ai safe to use during pregnancy? ⌄

Yes. Aayi.ai is recommended by your treating OB-GYN. All content is curated by qualified medical professionals and aligned to WHO and NMC antenatal guidelines. aayi Companion provides evidence-based answers only within its curated knowledge base — it never diagnoses or replaces your doctor.

How does aayi Companion know about my pregnancy? ⌄

Your doctor configures aayi Companion with your pregnancy details — gestational age, medical history, and specific notes. So when you ask a question, aayi Companion responds in context. A question about nausea at 8 weeks gets a different, more relevant answer than the same question at 30 weeks.

Does Aayi.ai replace my doctor? ⌄

No. Aayi.ai is a doctor-recommended companion, not a replacement. Everything you do in the app is visible to your treating doctor on their dashboard. If aayi Companion flags anything that needs clinical attention, your doctor is notified immediately.

Is my health data private and secure? ⌄

Yes. Your data is encrypted end-to-end and you are in full control. You decide what is shared with your doctor and for how long. Aayi.ai is compliant with India's DPDP Act and UAE PDPL. Your data is never sold or used for advertising.

Does Aayi.ai work without internet? ⌄

Yes. The core features — daily logging, aayi Companion cached responses, reminders, and checklists — work offline. Everything syncs automatically when connectivity is restored.
