FAQ for Doctors

What doctors ask before getting started

Does Aayi.ai work with my existing clinic software?⌄
Yes. Aayi.ai is built on HL7/FHIR interoperability standards and can integrate with most existing clinic management systems, EHRs, and lab systems via API. Our onboarding team handles the integration during your pilot setup.
How long does onboarding take?⌄
Most clinics are fully live within 48 hours. The onboarding process includes platform configuration for your specialty, a WhatsApp message template for inviting patients, and a 30-minute staff training session.
Do my patients need a smartphone?⌄
Patients need a basic Android or iOS smartphone. The Aayi app is designed to work on entry-level devices with minimal data usage. It also works offline, syncing when connectivity is available.
Is there a cost to my patients?⌄
Patient access to Aayi.ai is free when enrolled by a doctor. There are no costs passed to patients. The subscription is clinic-side only.
