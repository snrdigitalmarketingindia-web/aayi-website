The Pediatric Challenge

The vaccine gap is a public health problem

Missed vaccinations don't announce themselves. Parents forget. Life happens. By the time a child comes back for their next visit, the window for a critical dose may have passed — with no documentation of why.

Developmental milestones are harder to track. Each child is unique, but early identification of delays requires longitudinal data that most clinics simply don't have between appointments.

FAQ for Pediatricians

Common questions from pediatric practices

Does the vaccine schedule follow IAP or WHO guidelines?⌄
The default schedule follows IAP (Indian Academy of Pediatrics) recommended schedule for India, with WHO EPI schedule available for international practices. You can also customise the schedule for your specific clinical guidelines during onboarding.
Can parents see their child's vaccine record in the app?⌄
Yes. Parents have a dedicated view in the Aayi app showing their child's full vaccination history, upcoming doses, and a printable digital vaccine card. This reduces lost paper records significantly.
How does the milestone tracking work in practice?⌄
Parents receive age-appropriate milestone observation prompts in the app (e.g., "Can your baby sit without support?" at 6 months). Their responses are logged and compared against WHO milestone benchmarks. Any outliers appear on your dashboard with a flag for clinical review at the next visit.
Can I use Aayi.ai for both OB-GYN and Pediatric patients?⌄
Yes. Many Mother & Child Care hospitals and clinics use Aayi.ai for both specialties in a single account. The dashboard separates maternal and pediatric patient views, with specialty-specific features for each.
